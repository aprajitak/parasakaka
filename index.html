Achievements

The school has introduced initiatives such as ‘Education without seams for class 1’ and ‘daily activity period’ where students learn a variety of life, self-defence and sports skills. These initiatives ensure that the school keeps up with the needs of the day, and provides a holistic learning experience to the students.

our mission

  • school .

    Our unique CBSE based curriculum brings the best global learning trends to the classroom. We aim to nurture students who take pride in being Indian yet have a global outlook. The Paras World School is a place where children love to come, explore and learn!!.

  • School

    The Paras World School India, Gurugram ( TPWSI ), being among the best schools in Gurgaon, is committed to nurturing global learners who will be independent thinkers, successful learners, confident individuals & responsible citizens who are environmentally conscious & rooted in values. With focus on quality, we aspire to continuously innovate & enhance values. We believe that all our learners are unique & therefore need unique learning experiences & dynamic learning environment to ensure future readiness. Our task is to cultivate creative, joyful & compassionate inquirers who will use courageous and innovative thinking to build a sustainable world.
